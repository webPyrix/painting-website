Art Restoration & Conservation Studio

Based in Delhi - Since 2014

ARC studio is a team of dedicated conservators committed to the cause of fine art restoration with emphasis on preventive care and minimal intervention techniques.

Our Story

Founded in 2014, ARC Resources and Services is a fully-equipped private studio that provides highly skilled and efficient services in the field of art conservation. The ARC stands for Art Restoration and Conservation, and particularly takes care of an extensive range of art objects such as paintings, works on paper, sculpture, miniature paintings, prints, written media and contemporary materials.

We specialize in the conservation of Indian traditional, modern and contemporary works of art. Since its establishment, the studio has built a team of professionally trained and adept conservators.
